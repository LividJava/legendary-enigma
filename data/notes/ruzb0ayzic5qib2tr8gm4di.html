Holly is the current Location Manager for the Adventurer's Guild located in Duskalos. She can be a bit of an air-head at times, but is sharp and keen when it comes to reading a person's character. While a glutton for food, she runs a tight ship. Being the location manager for the budding town and organization. She out of necessity has Akko as a Close friend.
