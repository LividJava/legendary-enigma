Shelia's Camp

Type
Camp

A small fenced off, cozy, camp of survivors and friends. A small respite from the horrors of the world.

Foundation

The camp was founded by Akko and Shelia shorty after the destruction of their hometown Asterim. The pair decided to pool together resources and protect one another.

This camp would eventually become resolved and merge with Duskalos.
